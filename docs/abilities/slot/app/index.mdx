|  ONES   |
| :-----: |

## Overview

<Image img={require('./images/my-work.png')} />

The slots currently supported are as follows:

- [ones:workspace](#onesworkspace)
- [ones:project](#onesproject)
- [ones:wiki](#oneswiki)

## Slots

#### ones:workspace

**Module path：** My work

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:project

**Module path：** ONES Project

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:wiki

**Module path：** ONES Wiki

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:testcase

**Module path：** ONES TestCase

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:product

**Module path：** Product Management

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:desk

**Module path：** ONES Desk

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:deskhome

**Module path：** ONES DeskHome

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

This slot only can be accessed by the URL described below:

```
${location.protocol}://${location.host}/project/#/desk_home
```

#### ones:manhour

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:performance

**Module path：** ONES Performance

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:plan

**Module path：** ONES Plan

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:pipeline

**Module path：** ONES Pipeline

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:version

**Module path：** Version Management

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin, and up to 1 slots in the system.

#### ones:app:new

**Module path：** ONES left menu bar

**Number limit：** Declare a maximum of 1 slots in each plugin.

:::tip

- The name of the slot can be configured through the **title** attribute of modules in `plugin.yaml`
- The slot needs to be used with capabilities. For details, see: [Sidebar Menu](../../basic/app-menu.mdx)
- If you want to replace the whole right zone including the topbar zone, the `plugin.yaml` should be configured as follows:

  ```yaml
  modules:
    - id: ones-workspace-9rMc
      title: My work
      moduleType: ones:app:new
      entry: modules/ones-workspace-9rMc/index.html
  ```

  This has been supported since `ONES v3.16.8+`.

:::

## Usage

```yaml
modules:
  - id: ones-workspace-9rMc
    title: My work
    moduleType: ones:workspace
    modules:
      - id: ones-workspace-A0r3
        title: Overview
        entry: modules/ones-workspace-9rMc/ones-workspace-A0r3/index.html
```

**Supported config options：**

- [modules(required)](../../../reference/config/plugin.yaml#subModule)

:::caution

The `title` config cannot rename the name of the system sidebar menu, which should be configured in the page "Configuration" - "System preference settings" - "Sidebar menu".

:::
